## Design Pattern Applied

All three pages follow the same premium spacing reduction pattern:
- **Padding reduction**: ~25% smaller vertical padding on sections
- **Margin reduction**: ~50% smaller margins between elements
- **Gap reduction**: Reduced grid gaps from 12 to 8 units (or 8 to 6 units)
- **Icon sizing**: Reduced from 16x16 to 14x14 (or scaled proportionally)
- **Font sizing**: Each tier scaled down (text-4xl → text-3xl, etc.)
- **Text margins**: Reduced by 50% (mb-8 → mb-4, mb-6 → mb-3, etc.)
